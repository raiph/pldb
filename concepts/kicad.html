KiCad Legacy Layout is an open source application created in 1992.

#274on PLDB 32Years Old

KiCad (pronounced "Key-CAD") is a free software suite for electronic design automation (EDA). It facilitates the design of schematics for electronic circuits and their conversion to PCB designs. KiCad was originally developed by Jean-Pierre Charras. Read more on Wikipedia...
